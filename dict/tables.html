This a reference table for the Klingon writing system located on the inside of the front cover. It contains the romanized Klingon, printed pIqaD and an I.P.A. pronunciation guide. The punctuation characters (up- and downturned triangles) originates from the SkyBox trading cards, not everybody likes them. Next I hope to include Klingon longhand in this table.
