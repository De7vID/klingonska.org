Some Klingon GIF Fonts

A Somewhat Technical Issue

This page is included for the benefit of those people who are themselves creating or maintaining webpages with Klingon contents. - If you're not in the process of doing so, this information is not useful to you, and may be skipped altogether. The information contained in this page is in no way neccessary to understand the rest of Klingonska Akademien web page.  

To benefit from this page you should know at least some HTML. I use GIF fonts, to represent characters that are not normally available. Over the time there has become quite a few of them, each with its own purpose. Use this table to access them directly:  

Note: You may use these fonts freely, as long as you give me (Zrajm C Akfohg) credit and include a link to Klingonska Akademien webpage at http://come.to/klingonska/.

(Included because it's a pain to align text and pictures properly.)
ipa39pxlNo134-291bAn I.P.A. (International Phonetic Alphabet) transcription of pIqaD.
 

Unlike our own punctuation marks the Klingon ones are usually both followed and preceeded by a space, and may also occur at the beginning of a line [as appearing on the SkyBox Trading Cards S7, S8, S9, S13, S14 and S15]. The Klingon Empire symbol is sometimes used among klingonists to mark the end of a text or chapter [HolQeD].

Special symbols

 

These are my own inventions, not Klingon. I use to indicate that a syllable is an affix and not a word in it self, replaces the looked-up word in the dictionary, The slash is used to separate different words in for example a table.

Examples:

 


May your enemies run with fear.
[TKW p.65]
