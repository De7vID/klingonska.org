Archive of Okrandian Canon. (and Klingon Data Download)
There is now a search engine (BETA) for finding your way through the archive. This is an attempt at creating an archive of all existing okrandian canon. (A copy of Marc Okrand's The Klingon Dictionary is required to access transcriptions from the books and tapes.) [Changed 2007-08-17]
Klingon Pocket Dictionary. (on-line version)
Now completely re-written to reflect the status of my pocket dictionary. Come on in and take a look, you'll like it! [Changed 2007-08-06]
Errors in Okrandian Sources.
What errors are there in the The Klingon Dictionary? In the rest of the books? On the audio cassettes? And in the dialogue of the movies? When in doubt, check this list of known errors. [Changed 2007-07-15]
Qo'noS QonoS - KLI's journal in Klingon.
Taken directly from The Klingon Language Institute's web page and only slightly changed for better looking printouts. (With links back to the original page, of course.) [Changed 2007-07-15]
Some Songs in Klingon.
These are the lyrics for a few songs that I've heard (most of them at the qep'a' SochDIch). Hopefully I will be able to add musical notes and/or recordings of these songs to this section sometime soon. [Changed 2007-07-15]
pIqaD, And How to Read It. (and The pIqaD Playground)
Some stuff for the youthful mind, a writer's playground - type in any Klingon text to see it in pIqaD. An article about the Klingon alphabet and numbers, with pronounciation and orthography. (Punctuation and handwriting is not yet covered.) [Changed 2007-08-17]
Some pictures of pIqaD.
Pictures of interest. Paramount's version of pIqaD, a pIqaD typography example and a few examples of pIqaD handwriting. [Changed 2007-07-15]
Punctuation Marks in Okrandian Canon (and Klingon Punctuation Marks).
An attempt at analysing Marc Okrand's use of punctuation when writing Klingon and translations thereof. I've practically seased working on both of these articles, but maybe, some day... [Changed 2007-07-15]

Project KLO.
Översättningen av TKD till svenska. Projektets mål är en högkvalitativ översättning av och bearbetning av TKD till svenska förhållanden. Översättningen inkluderar bearbetning av materialet till svenska förhållanden (främst uttalsexemplen) och inkluderande av material tillkommet efter TKDs utgivande. Här finns också några tillägg bl.a. i form av en annorlunda affixguide, för att hjälpa dig på vägen. [Ändrad 2007-07-15]
