Songs in Klingon
These are the lyrics for a few songs that I’ve heard (most of them at the qep­’a’ Soch­DIch). Hopefully I will be able to add musical notes and/or recordings of these songs to this section sometime soon. [Changed 2007-07-15]
pI­qaD, And How to Read It (and The pI­qaD Playground)
Some stuff for the youthful mind, a writer’s playground – type in any Klingon text to see it in pI­qaD. An article about the Klingon alphabet and numbers, with pronounciation and orthography. (Punctuation and handwriting is not yet covered.) [Changed 2007-08-17]
Some Pictures of pI­qaD
Pictures of interest. Paramount’s version of pI­qaD, a pI­qaD typography example and a few examples of pI­qaD handwriting. [Changed 2007-07-15]
Punctuation Marks in Okrandian Canon (and Klingon Punctuation Marks)
An attempt at analysing Marc Okrand’s use of punctuation when writing Klingon and translations thereof. I’ve practically ceased working on both of these articles, but maybe, some day... [Changed 2007-07-15]
