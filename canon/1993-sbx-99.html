SkyBox Trading Card for DS9 Season 1

[Collector’s list of cards in this series]

One of the cards in the SkyBox trading card set for Star Trek: Deep Space Nine first season was produced in six different versions, with different languages: English (card 1), German (card 95), French (card 96), Spanish (card 97), Japanese (card 98), and Klingon (card 99). The Klingon was translation made by Marc Okrand. The Klingon card is here faithfully reproduced with all errors occuring in the original. For more details see the plain text transcription of card 99. (I've so far been unable to obtain a scan of the text of the English card, so that image is missing, and the text for that card is taken from Steven Boozer’s transcripts.)
