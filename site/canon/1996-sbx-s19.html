SkyBox Trading Cards for TNG Season 4

[Collector’s list of all cards in this series]

Three of the cards in the SkyBox trading card set for Star Trek: The Next Generation fourth season had Klingon text specially translated by Marc Okrand. They are here faithfully reproduced with all errors occuring in the originals. For more details on which these errors are, see the plain text transcriptions of card S19, card S20, and card S21. A transcript of these cards was also printed in HolQeD 5:2, page 14 in June 1996.

SkyBox Trading Card S19

S19: QumwI’ – Klingon Communicator

labmeH ’evnagh Se’ lo’ tlhIngan QumwI’. janmey
ngo’ lulo’lu’DI’ pIj jabbI’ID nISpu’ woj.

A Klingon™ communicator sends a signal through
subspace radio. Older models were susceptible to
radiation.

SkyBox Trading Card S21

yIntaHbogh tlhIngan Soj tlhol jablu’DI’ tIvqu’lu’. qagh,
