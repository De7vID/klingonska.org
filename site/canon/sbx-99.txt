SkyBox Trading Card for DS9 Season 1
====================================
<p class=center>[<a href="http://www.nslists.com/stdsn1.htm">Collector’s list
  of all cards in this series</a>]</p>

One of the cards in the SkyBox trading card set for *Star Trek: Deep Space
Nine* first season was produced in six different versions, with different
languages: English (card 1), German (card 95), French (card 96), Spanish (card
97), Japanese (card 98), and Klingon (card 99). The Klingon was translation
made by Marc Okrand. The Klingon card is here faithfully reproduced with all
errors occuring in the original. For more details on which these errors are,
see the plain text transcription of [card 99](1993-sbx-99.txt). (I’ve so far
been unable to obtain a copy [or scan of the text side] of the English card, so
that image is missing, and the transcript for that card is Steven Boozer’s, not
my own.) A transcript of card 99 was also printed in [*{HolQeD} 3:2,*
page 9](1994-06-holqed-03-2.txt) in <time datetime="1994-06">June 1994</time>.
