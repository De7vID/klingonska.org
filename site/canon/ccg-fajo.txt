Decipher Inc. Trading Card from  <i>The Fajo Collection</i>
===========================================================

<p class=center>[<a href="http://nslists.com/stccgfaj.htm">Collector’s list of
    all cards in this series</a>]</p>

One of the cards released in the <i>The Fajo Collection</i> of the [<i>Star
Trek Customizable Card
Game</i>](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek_Customizable_Card_Game) was
written entirely in Klingon (an English translation was, however, provided in
the rulebook accompanying the deck).

The text on the card consists of a description of how to use the card in game
play, copyright text, and some proverbs taken from <i>TKW.</i> The original
Klingon is of high quality and this have led people to speculate that it was
translated by Marc Okrand, but whether he was involved or not is not currently
known.

SUCCESS!

When threatened, fight. When in doubt, surprise them. Brute strength is not the
most important asset in a fight. Real power is in the heart. A Klingon does not
run away from his battles.

If an opponent attacks you, during that battle your Klingons with Honor add
INTEGRITY to STRENGTH. (Not cumulative.)
