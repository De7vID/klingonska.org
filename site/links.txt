Links to Other Pages About the Klingon Language
===============================================

If you know of or maintain a webpage that seem to bee suited for this page,
please mail me <i>[zrajm@klingonska.org](mailto:zrajm@klingonska.org)</i> so
that that we might add it. Be aware though, we're <i>only</i> interested in
pages that relate to the Klingon <i>Language</i>, such as on-line dictionaries,
fiction and poems written in {tlhIngan Hol}, language
resources, language courses etc. Anything in Klingon, or about the language
-- nothing else.
