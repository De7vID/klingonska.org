taHjaj wo’ – An Imperial Anthem

This song was written in 1993 by Rich Yampell, and has, as far as I know, been sung at all the qep’a’mey to date. To hear this song sung by a dozen or more klingonists in three-piece harmony is quite an experience!

There is also a parody of this song called DaHjaj po – Today morning.

Most of the files above are the result of me trying to transcribe the song to into musical score using Lilypond (a Linux note typesetter). As you may notice the melodies for voices II & III are missing (I hope to get back to this someday, but it might not happen anytime soon – I’ll be happy to accept your contribution!)
